## How payouts work

When you submit a withdrawal, SabiPay debits your wallet immediately and initiates a transfer to your linked account in the same request. There is no manual processing step.

- **Bank withdrawals** — funds are sent to your linked NGN bank account via NIBSS
- **Mobile money withdrawals** — funds are sent directly to your mobile money number

If the payout fails for any reason (invalid account, network error, insufficient balance), your wallet is refunded automatically and you'll receive a clear error message. Nothing is lost.

<Note>
Your wallet is debited at the moment you submit the withdrawal, not when the funds arrive. The transfer itself is processed asynchronously and typically arrives within minutes.
</Note>

## Before you can withdraw

1. **Link a bank account or mobile money number** — go to your dashboard under **Settings → Payout**
2. **For NGN bank accounts** — the account name is resolved automatically when you enter your account number. Confirm it matches before saving.
3. **Minimum withdrawal** — check your dashboard for the current minimum amount in your currency
