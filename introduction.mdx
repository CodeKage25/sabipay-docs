## What is SabiPay?

SabiPay is a payment infrastructure for businesses operating across Africa. Collect payments from customers via mobile money and card — from Nigeria to Senegal, Ghana, Kenya, Ivory Coast, and beyond — all through a single API.

## Supported Markets

| Country | Currency | Networks |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Nigeria | NGN | OPay |
| Ghana | GHS | MTN, AirtelTigo, Vodafone |
| Kenya | KES | M-Pesa |
| Senegal | XOF | Wave, Orange, Free, Wizall, Expresso |
| Ivory Coast | XOF | Orange, MTN, Moov, Wave |
| Togo | XOF | Moov, T-Money |
| Benin | XOF | MTN, Moov |
| Mali | XOF | Orange, Moov |
| Burkina Faso | XOF | Orange, Moov |

Card payments are supported in **USD** across all markets.
